Hi, I'm Cooper Maitoza.

I graduated from Southern Utah University with a bachelors of science in geology and a certification in geospatial information systems (GIS). I worked as an GIS Analyst for Kolob Canyon Air Services where I would create a the heat perimeter and classify different burn types for wildfires all over the western United States using ArcGIS Pro and Infrared imagery.

I currently work as a GIS Analyst for the roadway engineering company HDR where I convert CAD designs into Layouts, Web maps, and Dashboards for both the Public and Private sectors. I also help with Environmental Impact Statements and occasionally use Arcade, Python, and SQL dialects to help with automation and database management.

I have worked extensively with ESRI's products like Storymaps, Field Maps, and especially ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Online. I have experience in Python, SQL, and Arcade languages plus ArcGIS Pro Model builder. I built this website by myself (starting from a template) which further demonstrates my ability to rapidly learn new software, then implement it.
